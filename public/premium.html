Two clear modes

Choose how honest you want the AI to be.

Samba AI now has two premium paths. Direct AI is for users who want blunt, practical, no-nonsense answers. Truth AI is for users who want the assistant to stay extra careful, admit uncertainty, and correct mistakes directly instead of trying to please.

Launching in phases • Payments are not live yet

Simple difference

  • Direct AI: more direct tone, less pampering, stronger technical explanation, same normal chat flow.
  • Truth AI: stricter factual behavior, less guessing, stronger uncertainty handling, and clearer correction when something is wrong.
  • Both plans: premium model access, image/file help, and the same context-based hint support inside one conversation.
