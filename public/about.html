About Us

Welcome to Samba AI, a context-aware chat assistant built to help users learn clearly without losing the flow of a conversation.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make AI conversations easier to understand, especially when users get stuck on technical terms, unfamiliar ideas, or confusing explanations. We want people to continue the same main discussion and use short hint-style clarifications whenever they need help.

What We Do

Samba AI combines a main chat experience with context-aware hint support, so users can keep one clean conversation while still asking for quick explanations of difficult words or concepts. The platform also supports code help, file analysis, and multiple AI backends for different kinds of tasks.
